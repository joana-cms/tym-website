The Screen

Houses that Breathe

Web consulting and creation for therapists seeking a digital presence with soul and clarity.

Approach

Sites that don't shout, but breathe

Many care professionals feel the digital world is noisy, complicated, and impersonal. My mission is the opposite: to create structures that bring order to your thinking and function as an extension of your practice.

A good website is not just code. It is a bridge of trust between you and those seeking your care. I work to make that bridge clean, fast, and above all, authentic.

Methodology

How I Work

My process is collaborative and iterative. I don't just deliver a site; we build a digital home together that knows how to welcome your clients.

  • 1. Strategic Consulting: We define what is essential. Less is more.
  • 2. Soulful Design: We create a visual identity that conveys your essence.
  • 3. Performance & SEO: We ensure the site is fast and easy to find.

I use modern technologies that ensure client independence. The goal is for the site to be easy to manage, so you can focus on what really matters: people.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does creation take?

It depends on the complexity, but usually a medium project takes between 4 to 6 weeks from concept to launch.


Do I need to know about technology?

No. I handle the technical part and, at the end, provide you with a simple guide so you can make basic updates without fear.

What is required of me?

A willingness to share your vision and time to talk about your practice. Texts and images can be worked on together.


Does the site work on mobile?

Yes, absolutely. All sites are responsive and optimized for any device.
