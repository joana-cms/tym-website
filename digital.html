Methodology

How I Work

The process is collaborative, iterative, and highly personalized. I don't just deliver a site; we build together a flexible digital presence that knows how to welcome clients and highlight the differentiation of the work.

  • 1. Strategic Consulting: We define what is essential. Less is more.
  • 2. Soulful Design: We create a visual identity that conveys your essence.
  • 3. Performance & SEO: We ensure the site is fast and easy to find.

I use modern technologies that ensure client independence. The goal is for the site to be easy to manage, so you can focus on what really matters: people.

Doubts

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How long does creation take?

It depends on the complexity, but usually a medium project takes between 4 to 6 weeks from concept to launch.


Do I need to know about technology?

No. I handle the technical part and, at the end, provide you with a simple guide so you can make the basic updates without fear.

What is required of me?

A willingness to share a vision and time to talk about your practice. The texts and images can be worked on together.


Does the site work on mobile?

Yes, absolutely. All sites are responsive and optimized for any device.
