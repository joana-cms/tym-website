The Roots

About me

I've always loved movement. Sports, exploring the body, and discovering what really works. It was through yoga that I discovered Thai Yoga Massage — a practice that combines touch, stretching, and presence.

Over time, I realized I could also help therapists in another way: by creating simple, clear, and human websites where their work could be seen and understood without technical noise.

Today I do both. Two different ways of creating connection and opening space where there was once tension or invisibility.
